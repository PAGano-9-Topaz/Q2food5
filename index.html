Buko-Pandan

Step 1: 1 frozen pandan leaf sit at room temp until thawed

then tie into a knot

Step 2: place pandan knot, coconut water sugar, and agar powder

in a saucepan - stir frequently until agar and sugar dissolved

Step 3: add 1-2 drops of green fel food coloring - stir

Step 4: Remove leaf - pour mixture in pan, and let pan cool - 20min

Step 5: Refrigerate 1hr*30min get the gulaman slab, and cut into pieces

Step 6: place condensed milk, light cream, salt, and pandan extract

in a bowl, then whisk until smooth. Add gulaman - stir again

Step 7: get colander, pour nada-de-coco, kaong, and young coconut meat

Step 8: transfer colander items into gulaman bowl. fold, refrigerate, serve.

Ingredients

1 frozen pandan leaf‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎

1½ cups coconut water

½ cup granulated sugar

1 tsp agar-agar powder

1 or 2 drops green gel food coloring

1 14-ounce condensed milk

1 8 ounce light cream

¼ tsp kosher salt

¼ tsp pandan extract

1 jar nata de coco in syrup

1 jar kaong in syrup

1 can young coconut meat/pulp in syrup
